The typeof operator returns the type of the operand.
Syntax
You may also come across another syntax: typeof(x). It is the same as typeof x.
typeof is an operator, not a function.
The parentheses here aren’t a part of typeof.
It’s the kind of parentheses used for mathematical grouping.
Exception
The result of typeof null is "object".
That is an officially recognized error in typeof, coming from very early days of JavaScript and kept for compatibility.
Definitely, null is not an object. It is a special value with a separate type of its own.
The behavior of typeof is wrong here.
